# PRODUCT_VISION.md

## Product
Conversation Intelligence Platform

## One-line description
An AI platform that turns customer conversations into searchable, traceable, and actionable intelligence.

## ICP
Teams and companies that handle many customer interactions across calls, chats, and tickets.

## Daily users
- Support managers
- QA managers
- Operations
- Team leads
- Customer success
- Analysts

## Business value
The platform reduces time spent manually reviewing conversations, improves compliance monitoring, enables grounded Q&A over conversations, and provides coaching insights for agents.

## Core problem
Teams cannot efficiently search, review, understand, and act on large volumes of customer interactions.

## Differentiation
- Grounded answers with citations
- QA/compliance automation
- Searchable conversation intelligence
- Evaluation and feedback loop
- Production-style architecture, not a toy demo

## Critical workflow
1. Upload transcripts/chats/tickets
2. Process and normalize records
3. Chunk and index text
4. Search and ask questions
5. Show grounded answers with citations
6. Flag compliance/risk/coaching issues
7. Capture feedback and evaluate quality

## Must-have
- Upload and process conversation data
- Search and ask with citations
- QA/compliance checks
- Agent/coaching insight cards
- Evaluation layer

## Not now
- Multi-agent swarm
- Voice/audio ASR
- Full enterprise permissions
- Billing
- Large external integrations
